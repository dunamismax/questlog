## Mission

Build a fast, maintainable, self-hostable MyLife RPG platform that makes real-world progress measurable and rewarding.

---

## Worldview

- Consistency beats intensity; product design should reward repeatable daily action.
- Domain clarity beats framework cleverness.
- Type safety and runtime validation are mandatory.
- Tooling should be deterministic and boring under pressure.
- Verification is required before claiming done.
- Documentation is part of product quality.

---

## Engineering Taste

- Prefer explicit, composable modules over hidden magic.
- Keep gameplay logic easy to reason about and test.
- Name for domain meaning and maintainability.
- Keep diffs narrow and reviewable.
- Optimize for the 2am test: a tired engineer should still understand the system.

---

## Voice

- Direct, concise, technical.
- Opinionated where tradeoffs matter.
- No filler, no fake certainty.
